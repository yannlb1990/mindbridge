# Market Research & Competitive Analysis

## Executive Summary
The mental health practice management software market in Australia is fragmented, with most solutions either focusing on administrative functions (scheduling, billing) or clinical documentation, but rarely excelling at both while providing meaningful client engagement tools.

---

## Competitor Analysis

### 1. Heidi Health (Australia)
**Website**: heidihealth.com
**Founded**: 2019 (Melbourne)
**Funding**: $96.6M USD (Series A: $16.6M AUD in March 2025)

#### Strengths
- Industry-leading AI scribe technology
- 110+ language transcription
- Adaptive voice recognition learns clinician style
- Free tier available
- 8 million hours returned to clinicians annually
- 200+ specialties supported
- ISO 27001, HIPAA, GDPR, APP compliant

#### Features
- Real-time transcription during consultations
- Automatic progress note generation
- Referral letter generation
- Patient handout creation
- 1000+ templates across specialties
- EHR integrations (Best Practice, Epic, Athena)
- Custom template creation with AI assistance

#### Weaknesses
- Reports of "hallucinations" in transcriptions requiring verification
- EHR integration needs improvement (KLAS 2025)
- No client-facing portal
- General medical focus, not mental health specific
- No homework/exercise assignment features
- No eating disorder-specific tools

#### Pricing
- Free: Basic features, limited notes
- Pro: Custom templates, "Ask Heidi" feature

### 2. Zanda (formerly Power Diary) - Australia
**Website**: zandahealth.com

## Market Opportunity

### Gaps in Current Market
1. **No unified solution** combining AI scribe + practice management + client engagement
2. **Lack of mental health-specific AI** (most adapted from general medical)
3. **No eating disorder + general mental health** in one platform
4. **Youth mental health underserved** in digital tools
5. **Australian compliance often afterthought** (most US-focused)
6. **Client portals are transactional** (booking only), not therapeutic

### Target Market Size (Australia)
- 40,000+ registered psychologists
- 5,000+ psychiatrists
- 15,000+ mental health social workers
- 20,000+ counsellors
- Growing telehealth adoption post-COVID

### Key Differentiators for MindBridge
1. **Dual Platform**: Professional-grade clinician tools + therapeutic client space
2. **Australian-First**: Built for APP compliance, Medicare integration
3. **Specialty Focus**: Eating disorders, youth mental health, high-acuity
4. **Evidence-Based**: DBT, CBT, ACT, FBT frameworks embedded
5. **Safe Sharing**: Client can share photos, thoughts, journals in protected space
6. **Remote-Optimized**: Designed for telehealth-first practice
