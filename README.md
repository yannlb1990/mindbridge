# MindBridge - Australian Mental Health Practice Platform

## Vision
A comprehensive, dual-platform mental health solution designed specifically for the Australian market, connecting clinicians with clients in a secure, HIPAA/APP-compliant environment. Built to support remote psychology practice with a focus on eating disorders, youth mental health, and high-acuity cases.

## Target Users

### Clinicians
- Psychologists (Clinical, Counselling, Forensic)
- Psychiatrists
- Mental Health Social Workers
- Counsellors & Psychotherapists
- Eating Disorder Specialists
- Child & Adolescent Mental Health Practitioners

### Clients
- Adults with eating disorders (Anorexia Nervosa, Bulimia Nervosa, BED, ARFID)
- Children and adolescents (8-17 years) with mental health conditions
- High-acuity mental health clients requiring remote support
- Regional/rural Australians with limited access to in-person care
- Individuals preferring telehealth modalities

## Core Philosophy
- **Evidence-Based**: Built on CBT, DBT, ACT, and other validated therapeutic frameworks
- **Clinician-First UX**: Designed by psychologists, for psychologists
- **Safe Space**: Client portal as a therapeutic extension, not just a booking tool
- **Australian Compliance**: Full Privacy Act 1988 and APP compliance
- **Remote-First**: Enabling quality care regardless of geographic location

## Platform Components

### 1. MindBridge Clinician (Web App)
Professional practice management and clinical documentation

### 2. MindBridge Connect (Mobile App)
Client engagement, homework, and therapeutic exercises

### 3. MindBridge Admin (Web Portal)
Practice administration and multi-clinician management
